Sergii Pomian is an Polish-Ukranian artist (b. 1967, Tiraspol, Moldova) living in Nikolaev, Ukraine. In 1984–1988 he studied Fine Arts in the Grekov Odessa Art school. Together with the an Orthodox priest, he founded a workshop for icon painting, where he was the main artist and teacher for 5 years (2004-2009). Sergii has more than 25 years of experience in icon painting, participated in the painting of Orthodox and Catholic churches.

He went through an unusual path from a religious painting to a secular art. In the process of creative search his style evolved from the strict canon of icon painting to a more free and modern form of expression.

    STATEMENT

    “In my youth I was interested in ancient iconography, and then I realized that “the wind goes where it's pleasure takes it.”

    The traditional technique of icon painting and restoration allowed me to study on practice the method of the old masters.

    I try to combine traditions and modern vision. I'm interested in the line between figurative and abstraction, the relationship between form and background, patterns presence.

    In my painting I explore the dimension of irony, humor and melancholy.”
